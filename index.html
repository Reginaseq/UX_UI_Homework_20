Overview

Where can one got to support families and individuals living without the internet?

The Problem

Having access to internet in 2021 is an essential need to succeed the Digital Divide.

19 million American’s still lack access to fixed broadband service at threshold speeds.

Donors need a trustworthy place to donate to help close the Digital Divide.

The Solution

EveryoneOn is a non-profit organization that has stepped in to close our nation’s Digital Divide.

EveryoneOn website lacks of presence and clear message. Creating hesitancy for potential donors.

Our team set out to improve content quality and structure. by making these changes we individual donations could increase from 1.65% to 3%.
